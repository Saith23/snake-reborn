Changelog Alpha 0.0.2

New things

Separated the game into 3 different rooms

Main

Apple: Comes in red and green, both gives 1 score.

Poison: Slowly takes away 5 score.

Rose: Heals you.

Outer Space

The Sun: triggers an escape sequence lasting 5 seconds.

Neutron: Gives a random amount of score between -2 and 2. Teleports away after consumed.

Black hole: Aimlessly moves, kills on touch.

The Fields

Wall: Teleports you back to the start.

Thorn: Takes away a life, and teleports you back to the start.

Treasure: Gives an immense amount of score, locks you out.

There is now a personal best.

Buttons now have new transition colors.

Buttons' transitions take longer.

New button: Reset Highscore. Resets your personal best.

Changes

Temporarily removed gold (hinted from the multiplier thing still existing)

Removed a lot of foods.

Smoothed out the outlines for the buttons.

Slightly reworked the background color.

Changed the colors for some buttons.

In Outer Space, the snake now wears a suit.

Negative score is no longer fatal.

Nerfed the speed of Rose

Poison now actually poisons you, instead of just taking away a lot of score at once.
